Becky The Giant Raccoon

Published 7/14/2020




Becky was a rescued after sustaining an injury that made it difficult for her to walk. After spending some time with an elderly woman, her size grew to be tremendous due to too many pistachios and marshmallows. She quickly went viral after a video of an obese raccoon coming out of a little house made the rounds on Facebook. Fortunately, Becky the Raccoon is much healthier now and helps educate people about wildlife rescues.
