What is this place?

A curiosity blog that pulls interesting content from all over the web into one place. Turbolyzer.com is run by two graduate students with too much time on their hands.


Can I submit content to be featured?

Yes! Send us a link through our Contact Us page.


Interested in working with us?

We're looking to collaborate with programmers, web developers and content producers. Shoot us a message through our Contact form.


Permissions

Any pictures or videos that aren't pulled from YouTube or a post on Social media (Twitter, Instagram, etc) are being used with direct permission from the original source. We are against directly publishing people's content without their permission, so please reach out to us with any concerns through our Contact form.

We do not store or sell any personal data.




Turbolyzer.com is an amazon affiliate and we earn from qualifying purchases on our Shop section.
